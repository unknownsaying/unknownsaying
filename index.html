About Us

Forged in Darkness, Driven by Passion

DarkGameStudio was founded in 2020 by a small team of developers who believe the best stories are told in the shadows. We blend atmospheric design with tight gameplay to create unforgettable experiences.

Based in Seattle, our fully remote team spans 8 countries. We value creative freedom, meticulous world-building, and the power of a well-told story.
